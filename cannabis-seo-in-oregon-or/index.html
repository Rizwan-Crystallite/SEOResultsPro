Implementing Cannabis SEO Strategies

Keyword Research

When you're doing CBD SEO in Oregon, the first step is keyword research. This means finding the words people use when they search for cannabis products and services in Oregon. Here's how you do it:

  • Use Tools: Online tools like Semrush or Ahrefs can help you find the right keywords. They show you what words people search for and how many look them up.
  • Think Like Your Customer: Put yourself in your customer's shoes. What would they type in Google if they were looking for what you offer? Maybe it's "best cannabis in Oregon" or "Oregon cannabis store near me".
  • Pick the Best Keywords: Look for keywords that lots of people search for, but only a few websites are using. These are hidden treasures that can make your website more visible.

On-Page SEO Techniques

Once you have your keywords, you must use them on your website. This is called on-page SEO. Here are some tips:

  • Use Keywords Naturally: Add your keywords to your website's text, like in articles, product descriptions, and titles. But don't overdo it. It should sound natural.
  • Meta Tags and Descriptions: These parts of your website's code tell search engines what your website is about. Make sure they include your keywords.
  • Images: Use pictures related to cannabis and include your keywords in the file names and the 'alt text' (a description of the image for search engines).

Off-Page SEO Techniques

Off-page SEO is about what happens outside your website. Here's what you can do:

  • Backlinks: These are links from other websites to yours. Try to get links from good-quality websites. It's like a vote of confidence that tells search engines your site is trustworthy.
  • Google Business Profile: If you have a cannabis shop, make sure your Google Business Profile is updated. Include your address, hours, and photos. Good reviews here can also help SEO.
