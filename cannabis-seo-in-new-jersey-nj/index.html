Key components of SEO: On-page, Off-page, and Technical SEO


SEO has three main parts:
  • On-page SEO is about the content on your website. It includes things like the keywords you use and how you organize them.
  • Off-page SEO is what happens away from your website, like backlinks from other websites to yours.
  • Technical SEO is about how well your website works. It includes things like how fast your pages load and if your website is easy to use on mobile phones.
