Cannabis Legality and Regulations in Florida

In Florida, the cannabis story is interesting. Medical cannabis is legal, but recreational use isn't – yet. This means people with health issues can use cannabis if they have a doctor's permission.

But you can't just buy it for fun. Florida has strict rules about who can sell cannabis and where. If you're doing SEO for cannabis, remember, you're talking to people who need it for health reasons, not just anyone.
