CBD SEO Basics for Dispensaries

What is SEO For Cannabis and Why Does It Matters?

SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimization." It's a way to make your dispensary's website easier to find when people search for it online.

Think of SEO for cannabis, like giving directions to your shop. The better the directions, the more people can find your shop without getting lost. If you do SEO right, your shop shows up at the top when people look for where to buy cannabis in Colorado.

The Parts of SEO: On-Page, Off-Page, and Tech Stuff

There are three big pieces to SEO:

  • On-Page SEO: This is all about what's on your website. It's like making sure your online shop is neat so when people come in, they can find what they want easily. You use the right words that people search for, put up good pictures, and write helpful info about your products.
  • Off-Page SEO: This is about getting other places to talk about your shop. It's like if many friends tell other friends how great your shop is. This can be other websites linking to you or people on social media sharing your stuff.
  • Technical SEO: This is the behind-the-scenes work to ensure your website works well. This means your website loads fast, works on phones and doesn't have broken pages that lead nowhere.

Know Who You're Talking To and What They Want

Before you do anything, you need to think about who is coming to your shop and why. What are they looking for? Some might want something to help them relax, others might be curious and new to cannabis. By understanding your customers, you can use the right words and create the right kind of content on your website that speaks to them.

With these basics, you can start planning for your dispensary's website. It's about being clear, and helpful, and making sure people can find you when they're looking for what you sell.

Like a well-located and well-known shop in town, a well-optimized website can draw in more customers looking for cannabis in Colorado.

Keyword Research for Cannabis SEO In Colorado

Why Finding the Right Words is Like a Treasure Hunt

Think of keyword research like a treasure hunt. In this hunt, the treasure is the words people type into search engines when looking for a place to buy cannabis.

When you know these special words, you can use them on your website to help people find your shop. This is important because using the right words can lead more people to your website.

Tools and Tricks to Find These Special Words

To find these treasure words, or "keywords," you can use tools on the internet. Some tools are free, like Google's Keyword Planner, and some cost money.
