15 Innovative Approaches Your Local SEO Agency Should Be Using

By Seo Results Pro on June, 2023

Staying ahead of the curve is paramount for achieving success. One area that continually sees evolution and demands fresh, innovative strategies is Local SEO. Given the increasing importance of local businesses in our communities and economies, it's vital for your Local SEO agency to embrace the latest techniques and approaches. Here, we delve into the 18 pioneering strategies that your Local SEO agency should be incorporating into their game plan.

The Ever-Evolving Landscape of Local SEO

Local SEO has always been about connecting local businesses with local customers. However, with technological advancements and shifts in consumer behavior, the means to make this connection have become more sophisticated. It's not just about being found anymore; it's about being found by the right people at the right time and in the right context.

15 Strategies That Your Local SEO Agency Should Be Incorporating

1. Hyperlocal Targeting: Beyond the Basics

In the realm of Local SEO, one approach that's gaining traction is hyperlocal targeting. While 'local' might mean an entire city or a region for some businesses, 'hyperlocal' narrows this down even further - think neighborhoods or even specific streets. This intense focus allows businesses to cater their online presence to a very defined audience.

For example, a cafe in the artsy district of a city could tailor its content to resonate with the artistic community, discussing local art events, featuring local artists, or even using neighborhood-specific jargon. This strategy ensures that the cafe doesn't just attract online traffic, but traffic that's most likely to walk through its doors.

2. Engaging with Voice Search

The rise of smart assistants like Alexa, Siri, and Google Assistant has led to a surge in voice searches. These searches are typically more conversational and can differ from what someone might type into a search bar. Your Local SEO agency should be optimizing content to account for these types of searches. This often involves focusing on long-tail keywords and natural language, ensuring local businesses appear in search results when potential customers are conversing with their smart devices.

3. Understanding and Utilizing Entity Search

Search engines are becoming smarter. They no longer just look at keywords but understand entities and their relationships. For Local SEO, this means that search engines can understand that a pizzeria, a pizza shop, and a pizza cafe might all refer to the same type of establishment. Your Local SEO agency should be aware of this and optimize content to ensure that it's aligned with this entity-based approach, weaving a comprehensive web of related terms and concepts.

4. Deep Dive into Local Link Building

Link building remains a cornerstone of SEO. For Local SEO, the focus should be on establishing links from reputable local sources. Think local news websites, community blogs, or partnership pages of nearby businesses. These local links serve a dual purpose: they reinforce the business's standing in the community and send strong local signals to search engines.

5. A Shift Towards Visual Searches

With platforms like Pinterest and Google Lens allowing users to search using images, visual search is on the rise. Local businesses, especially those in sectors like fashion, decor, or food, can benefit immensely by optimizing for visual searches. This involves ensuring high-quality images, appropriate alt texts, and structured data to provide context to these images.

6. Mobile-First Indexing

The advent of mobile-first indexing by Google has fundamentally changed the SEO landscape. This approach prioritizes the mobile version of a website for ranking and indexing, given the increasing number of mobile users. A responsive and mobile-friendly website is no longer just an option; it's a necessity.

Your Local SEO agency should ensure local businesses have websites optimized for mobile-first indexing. This means fast-loading, responsive designs that provide an exceptional user experience on smartphones and tablets. Google's algorithms are now geared towards rewarding websites that prioritize mobile users, which has significant implications for local businesses trying to attract customers.

7. Leveraging User-Generated Content

User-generated content, such as reviews and social media interactions, plays a substantial role in shaping a local business's online reputation. Local SEO agencies should encourage their clients to engage with customers on social media actively, respond to reviews, and even feature user-generated content on their websites.

Not only does this create a sense of community and trust, but it also sends positive signals to search engines. Fresh and relevant user-generated content can enhance a business's visibility in local search results.

8. Local Structured Data Markup

Structured data markup helps search engines understand the content on a website better. For local businesses, implementing structured data markup can be a game-changer. It enables businesses to provide detailed information about their products, services, and even upcoming events to search engines directly.

For instance, a local restaurant can use structured data to highlight its menu items, prices, and customer reviews in search results. This rich information makes the listing more appealing to potential customers and can improve click-through rates.

9. AI and Machine Learning Integration

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) have revolutionized the SEO world. Search engines like Google are using these technologies to understand user intent better and deliver more relevant search results. Your Local SEO agency should harness AI and ML tools to gain insights into local search trends, customer behavior, and competitor analysis.

For instance, AI-powered chatbots can respond immediately to customer queries on a local business's website. These bots can be customized to provide information about store hours, product availability, and even book appointments. Such interactions enhance the user experience and gather valuable data for further optimization.

10. Localization of Content and Keywords

Localization goes beyond merely adding a location tag to content. It involves creating content that resonates with the specific local audience, using phrases, terms and references unique to the region. Your Local SEO agency should be adept at crafting localized content strategies that capture the essence of the local culture and community.

For example, a local brewery might create content that discusses the history of brewing in the area, local beer festivals, and partnerships with nearby businesses. This type of content not only enhances the business's local identity but also appeals to local customers.

11. Collaborative Content Marketing

Content marketing and collaboration can be a game-changer in the world of local SEO. Local SEO agencies should encourage their clients to collaborate with other local businesses or influencers to create engaging content.

For instance, a local coffee shop could partner with a nearby bakery to create a joint blog post about the perfect coffee and pastry pairings. Such collaborations not only expand the content reach but also strengthen community ties and attract a broader local audience.

12. Local SEO Meets Social Commerce

The intersection of Local SEO and social commerce presents an exciting opportunity. Social media platforms have increasingly become shopping destinations, and local businesses should leverage this trend. Local SEO agencies can guide businesses in optimizing their social media profiles for local search.

Platforms like Instagram and Facebook offer location-based features that allow businesses to tag their physical locations in posts. Additionally, they enable the creation of shoppable posts, where users can purchase products directly from a post. Integrating these features into a local business's social media strategy can simultaneously drive foot traffic and online sales.

13. Personalization Through AI

Artificial intelligence is not limited to chatbots; it can also be used to personalize the user experience. Local SEO agencies should explore AI-driven personalization strategies that tailor content and recommendations to individual users based on their browsing history and preferences.

For instance, an e-commerce business can use AI to suggest products based on a user's past purchases and browsing behavior. Businesses can increase customer satisfaction and drive more conversions by providing personalized recommendations.

14. Predictive Analytics for Local SEO

Predictive analytics uses historical data and machine learning algorithms to forecast future trends and behaviors. Local SEO agencies can leverage predictive analytics to anticipate changes in search behavior and adjust their strategies accordingly.

For instance, predictive analytics can help businesses identify peak times for local searches or seasonal trends. This information can guide the timing of marketing campaigns, ensuring that businesses are prepared for surges in demand during specific times of the year.

15. Google Business Profile Optimization

Optimizing your Google Business Profile is vital for local visibility. Ensure accurate information, high-quality images, and proactive review management. Your Local SEO agency leverages Google Posts to engage customers and enhance local SEO.

Conclusion

The world of Local SEO is richer and more varied than ever before. As technology evolves and consumer behaviors shift, it's vital for Local SEO agencies to stay ahead of the curve. Incorporating innovative approaches like hyperlocal targeting, voice search optimization and visual search are no longer optional but essential.

Local businesses are the lifeblood of communities, offering goods and services and adding to the local flavor and culture. Ensuring they remain visible and relevant in online searches is a task that requires constant innovation, agility, and foresight. It's a task that your Local SEO agency should be equipped for, ensuring that local businesses survive and thrive in this digital age.
